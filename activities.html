Activities

There are many things to do as an exchange student in Christchurch, but here are a few of the most popular ones:

Skiing

1. Mt St Hut Ski Area

Voted New Zealand's Best Ski Resort for 8 years in a row, Mt Hutt ski area boasts wide-open terrain for all ski and snowboard ability levels.

2. Roundhill Ski Area

Roundhill Ski Area is a family owned ski area with breathtaking views of Lake Tekapo and the Southern Alps.

Note: this ski area is not very beginner friendly since it doesn't have much beginner ski areas,

so if you want to ski here, just make sure that you have some experience in skiing.

3. Broken River Ski Area

Broken River is a club skifield in South Island, New Zealand. Run by the Broken River Ski Club, it is located at about 110 km (68 mi) from Christchurch.

The skifield claims to often have the longest ski season anywhere in Australasia. Also, by connecting with Craigieburn Valley, it claims to have the largest skiiable area in New Zealand.

Surfing

1. Sumner Beach

The Sumner Beach is a good place for surfers of all skill levels. Even if you don't go surfing at that beach, the view is still very nice!

2. Taylors Mistake

Taylors Mistake is a very popular beach in Christchurch and home to some of the best surf in the city.

Taylors Mistake is a beach break on a sandy beach with both right and left-handers on offer.

Taylors can produce a really powerful wave, with heaps of speed off the drop and barrels when offshore.

This beach is suitable for surfers of all skill levels.

3. New Brighton Pier

New Brighton is one of Christchurch's local surfing beaches. Its a long beach with many peaks down

It's good for surfers of all skill levels.

Tourist attractions

1. International Antarctic Centre

The International Antarctic Centre is a visitor attraction and public science discovery centre in the suburb of Harewood, Christchurch, New Zealand.

It is located at Christchurch International Airport, adjacent to the buildings that are the Christchurch base for the Antarctic programmes of the US, New Zealand and Italy.

2. Christchurch Tram

One of the most notable things about Christchurch. It combines both history and sightseeing together, creating a unique experience.

3. Punting On The Avon

This is a boat trip on the Avon River which allows you to go sightseeing in Christchurch in a completely different perspective.
